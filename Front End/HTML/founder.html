The Founder

A Message From the Founder

"An officer isn't selected in five days at the Board. He is built, decision by decision, long before he walks in."

After 24 years in the Indian Army, including a posting as an Assessor at the Services Selection Board, I founded GVC Sainik SSB Academy to close the gap between coaching that teaches answers and training that builds Officer Like Qualities.

Every mentor on our faculty has worn the uniform or assessed candidates who have. We don't run a tuition centre — we run a Board rehearsal, five days a week, twelve months a year.
