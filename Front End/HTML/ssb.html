SSB Process

Five Days, Five Stages

1

Screening

OIR test & PPDT on Day 1. Roughly 1 in 5 candidates clear.

2

Psychology

TAT, WAT, SRT and Self-Description on Day 2.

3

GTO Tasks

GD, Command Task, Group Obstacles across Day 3–4.

4

Interview

Personal interview run in parallel with GTO days.

5

Conference

Final board review and recommendation on Day 5.
